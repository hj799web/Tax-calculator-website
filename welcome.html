Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the calculator?

Our calculator uses the latest 2024 tax rates and brackets for all provinces and territories in Canada. While it provides a close estimate based on the information you provide, it's designed for educational and planning purposes. For personalized tax advice, please consult a tax professional.

Is my data secure?

Yes! All calculations are performed in your browser. We don't store any of your personal or financial information on our servers.

How often is the calculator updated?

We update the calculator annually to reflect changes in tax rates, brackets, and government spending allocations. The current version uses 2024 tax rates.

Can I use the calculator for business taxes?

The calculator is designed for personal income taxes. Business taxes involve different rules and considerations.
